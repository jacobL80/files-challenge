Vel'Koz

Vel'Koz is a non-humanoid creature that comes from the 'Void' in the PC game League of Legends. He came to Runeterra in search of knowledge, and will allow nothing to come between him and his goal. He is vastly intelligent and, through unethical methods, absorbs all sorts of information from his surroundings.

Vel'Koz is a champion in The League of Legends.
